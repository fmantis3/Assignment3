SUNFLOWERS

Definition: A tall North American plant of the daisy family, with very large golden-rayed flowers. Sunflowers are cultivated for their edible seeds, which are an important source of oil for cooking and margarine.

Three facts about Sunflowers

  • They are native to the Americas
  • Sunflowers move with the Sun
  • The World's tallest Sunflower reaaches 30 feet and 1 inch
